How to make an Omellete?

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Recipe to cook the omellete

  1. Take 3 eggs in a bowl, add salt to them and then beat them with a fork.
  2. Melt the butter over a medium-low heat in a nonstick skillet.
  3. Add beaten eggs to the skillet.
  4. Leave the eggs for a minute and then use a spatula to cook evenly.
  5. Add the cheese to the centre and let it soften.
  6. Fold the omellete with spatula and serve.
